Naturalist Games

Naturalist Games is an independent board game publisher. They needed a single page site that was succinct, responsive, and directed users to their game listings and social media accounts. Additionally, they needed a bright color scheme that reflected their branding. I developed the site, wrote the copy, and designed the images used on the site.
